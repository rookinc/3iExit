JPL HORIZONS HOWTO — vectors for 3iExit
=======================================
Goal: export heliocentric vectors (Sun-centered) for Earth and 3I/ATLAS, Ecliptic J2000, units AU, 1-hour cadence.
Steps:
1) Open JPL Horizons → 'Vector Table'.
2) Target: Earth; Center: Sun [10]; Frame: Ecliptic & Mean Equinox of Reference Epoch (J2000); Units: AU; Step: 1 h.
3) Export plain text. Do the same for 3I/ATLAS.
4) Convert to CSV or use the parser from earlier notebook scripts.
